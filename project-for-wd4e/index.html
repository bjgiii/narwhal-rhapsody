The Narwal Rhapsody

The adventuring crew zoom across the Astral Sea in a ship called the Narwal Rhapsody. She is 35 feet long, and 20 feet wide at her largest point. Roughly in the shape of a nawhal, she also sports a diamond tipped spear at her hull which can be used to ram into things, in a dire situation.

AT0-M1

AT0-M1 is a robot gnome who builds smaller robots. He is accompanied by a small sentient anvil, Anvillio. AT0-M1 is somewhat chaotic, but always quick to protect others. His arms change into whatever tool is necessary to finish whatever job comes his way!


Doctor Pilot Evelyn Smasher

Doctor Pilot Evelyn Smasher is an Astral Elf doctor and pilot from Tarturus IV. She has five PhD's: in Medicine, Exobiology, Nuclear Physics, Astronomy, and Alchemy. She is a master of science and magic and is extremely intelligent. She is extremely cunning and quick to protect others, but often more concerned with her diamonds than anything else.


Spark

Spark is what's called a Helper - a group of autognomes created and made to help the multiverse. They are a helper at heart - always quick to provide any assistance possible. He carries a beautiful staff with a dodecahedral crystal set in its head. Spark is a Cleric: a master of the healing and mystical arts of the gods.


Duvors D'Fancèlands

Duvors D'Fancèlands is a Bard of Lore from the Fey Wilds. He is a hobgoblin who masquerades as a member of the court of Summer, when in actuality he is a spy for the Winter courts! He loves intrigue, disuguises, mayhem, and music.


Sale

Sale is a mysterious dwarfish compainon of ours. Our crew found him hard at work on an astroid world and convinced him to come adventuring with us after learning he'd never had a vacation before!
